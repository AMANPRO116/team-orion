TEAM ORION

Next-generation stealth UAV platform

Introduction

UAVs are critical in modern defense systems. Key requirements include:

  • Low observability
  • High aerodynamic efficiency
  • Long endurance

This project focuses on a stealth UAV platform inspired by advanced fighter aerodynamics.

Design Inspiration

  • Inspired by F-35 Lightning II
  • Edge alignment
  • Blended Wing Body (BWB)
  • Reduced vertical surfaces
  • Adapted for subsonic UAV operation

Objective

  • Design a stealth-inspired UAV
  • Reach subsonic speeds
  • Applications:
    • Cargo
    • Stealth missions
    • Surveillance
    • Emergency medical

Theoretical Calculations

Lift = 220.5 N

Drag = 13.78 N

L/D Ratio = 16

Reynolds Number ≈ 1.02 × 10⁶

Physical Model

CG and COM are yet to be determined due to pending material weight estimation.

Simulations

External flow domain created (~5–10× UAV length)

Boundary Conditions

  • Velocity Inlet
  • Pressure Outlet
  • No-slip wall (UAV surface)

Wing Profile (NACA 23012)

  • Moderate camber → lift at low AoA
  • 12% thickness → strength
  • Low pitching moment
  • Good stall behavior

Propulsion System

  • Front EDF: 30° tilt
  • Rear EDFs: swivel duct

Emergency Detaching System

Front module detaches during failures or threats to preserve critical data. Ensures controlled descent and recovery of mission intelligence.

System Modes

  • Combined Mode (VTOL hybrid)
  • Separated Mode (two UAVs)

Electrical Architecture

  • Dual independent power systems
  • Front & Rear modules operate independently

Power & Propulsion

  • Separate batteries
  • Front EDF + Dual rear EDFs
  • Independent ESC control

Avionics & Communication

  • Master-slave flight controllers
  • Wired (UART/CAN) + Wireless (LoRa)
  • Data exchange: thrust, tilt, flight mode

Control System

  • Rear FC = Master
  • Front FC = Executor
  • Thrust vectoring with tilt control

Safety System (CAASS)

  • Monitors thrust, tilt, IMU
  • Detects failures & instability
  • Actions: abort, hover, safe mode

Failsafe & Integration

  • Dual battery safety
  • Communication monitoring
  • Independent UAV capability
