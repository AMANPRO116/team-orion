Introduction

UAVs are critical in modern defense systems

Key requirements:

  • Low observability
  • High aerodynamic efficiency
  • Long endurance

This work focuses on a stealth UAV platform inspired by advanced fighter aerodynamics

Design Inspiration

Inspired by F-35 Lightning II

  • Edge alignment
  • Blended wing-body (BWB)
  • Reduced vertical surfaces
  • Adapted for subsonic UAV operation

Objectives

  • Design a stealth-inspired UAV
  • Reach sub-sonic speeds
  • Cargo, surveillance & emergency medical applications
